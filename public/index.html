Aas ~ Never lose hope!

Aas is an attempt where a women in distress finds help in another woman who lends her the right advice to fight back. We have women that voulunteer as advisors for the cause of womanhood- there are lawyers, doctors, social servants, teachers or even survivors of abuse, violence or anyother atrocity that are in best position to lend a helping hand.


Our initiative springs from the fact that in a country like india, where most women are taught to adjust, keep quiet and tolerate hoping things would turn right someday, they can't voice anything. There are times when even in the best of most educated families, these women have no one to share their pains with.
Loss of family reputation often holds them back from speaking out loud.

Aas lends all distressed women, a ray of hope. It sends out a strog message that you must not lose hope
